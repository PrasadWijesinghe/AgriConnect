# 🌾 Agri Connect – Smart Agriculture Digital Platform

**Agri Connect** is a digital platform designed to empower **farmers and agricultural entrepreneurs** by providing **smart crop recommendations, AI-driven agricultural guidance, and multiple crop-selling marketplaces** using real-time market insights.

This system connects **farmers, harvest buyers, and harvest requesters** to ensure fair pricing, better decision-making, and improved agricultural productivity.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🌱 Smart Crop Recommendation

* Location-based crop suggestions (e.g., Kandy, Colombo, etc.)
* Planting month analysis for optimal yield
* Data-driven recommendations for maximum profitability

### 🤖 AI Agriculture Chatbot

Farmers can interact with an intelligent chatbot to get:

* Crop cultivation techniques
* Irrigation planning and fertilizer guidance
* Disease detection and prevention methods
* Seasonal farming tips and best practices

### 🛒 Crop Selling Marketplaces

Farmers can sell harvested crops using **two flexible selling modes**:

#### 1️⃣ Bidding Marketplace

* Buyers place competitive bids
* Highest bidder wins
* Ensures best possible price for farmers

#### 2️⃣ Real-Time Marketplace

* Displays current market prices
* Prices shown by city (Kandy, Colombo, etc.)
* Farmers can instantly sell at market rate

### 📊 Market Insights

* Real-time crop price updates
* Location-based market comparison
* Helps farmers choose the best selling option

### 📦 Inventory & Income Management

* Track harvested crop inventory
* Monitor sales and earnings
* View income history and performance analytics

### 📥 Harvest Requests

* Buyers can post harvest requests
* Farmers can respond directly
* Faster and more transparent trade process

---

## 👤 User Roles

### 👨‍🌾 Farmers / Agricultural Entrepreneurs

Farmers can:

* Get crop recommendations based on location & season
* Use AI chatbot for expert agricultural guidance
* Sell crops via bidding or real-time marketplaces
* View real-time crop prices
* Respond to buyer harvest requests
* Manage inventory and track income

### 🏪 Buyers / Harvest Requesters

Buyers can:

* Post harvest requests
* Participate in bidding marketplace
* Purchase crops at real-time market prices
* Connect directly with farmers
